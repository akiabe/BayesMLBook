多次元ガウス分布¶

  • $$\mathcal{N}(\mathbf{x} | \mathbf{\mu}, \mathbf{\Sigma}) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{(2\pi)^D |\Sigma|}} \exp\Bigr\{-\frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{\mu})^T \mathbf{\Sigma}^{-1} (\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{\mu}) \Bigr\} $$ $$\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^D \quad \mathbf{\mu} \in \mathbb{R}^D \quad \mathbf{\Sigma} \in \mathbb{R}^{D \times D}$$

  • $$\ln \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{x} | \mathbf{\mu}, \mathbf{\Sigma}) = -\frac{1}{2}\Bigr\{(\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{\mu})^T \mathbf{\Sigma}^{-1} (\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{\mu}) + \ln |\mathbf{\Sigma}| + D\ln2\pi \Bigr\}$$
